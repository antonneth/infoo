Welcome to Camber Club

Camber Club is a collection of 1000 NFTs called "Cambers", all NFTs are individually illustrated by Ian Tang. This is not only an art NFT project. Every camber gives you access to an exclusive membership that grants you entries to sick live events, whitelist for future collections , NFT airdrops, a only-holders Discord server, car meets , in-game meets and much more that will be revealed in our Concept Map...

What is Camber?

Camber refers to the angle of the wheel and tire relative to a perfectly flat road. There are 2 camber variations: In positive camber the wheel leans towards the interior of the car, and the negative camber leans in the exterior of the car which our NFTs have.
